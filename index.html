Innovation in Informatin Technology builds on the principles of Business processes.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What role does Technology play in business?

    Technology's role has changed over the years, however, from merely operational to highly strategic. Companies are designing their IT roadmaps to drive goals, innovate and lead change in their industries.

  • What is managed IT and its role in small businesses?

    This involves a business partnering with an outside IT company to oversee and manage all of their technology (and sometimes overall company) needs. It is used in small businesses as a replacement for or suppliment to an internal IT person or team.

  • Will computers and advancements in IT take away my job?

    On a broad scale - probably not! We're of the mindset that computers and advancements in AI will take over the boring, tedious, repetetive parts of our jobs. Humans will be left to tackle the more creative, strategic tasks that computers aren't great at.

  • What is tech/IT support in a business?

    Technically speaking it is the technical help or knowledge provided by computing experts to businesses .. a team or department in a company or organisation that provides technical help to people who have problems with its computer systems.

  • How much do companies spend on Information Technology?

    As of 2017, the average for IT spending across industries was about 3% of company revenue according to Deloitte's Global CIO Survey. This average does vary heavily depending on industry, company size, location, and the value an individual business places on technology.

  • What are the factors to consider when choosing an IT Company?

    Most of the services provided by IT companies are just the same . We have the managed IT service, the on-demand IT, network setup, and network security services, among others. But the best IT companies differentiate themselves interms of Reliability and Security, Fast Customer Service, Specialization, Customer reviews, and Insurance.
